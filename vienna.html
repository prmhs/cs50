Vienna ist the capital of Austria. About two million people out of Austria's population of eight million live in Vienna. The danube river flows through the city. There are many parks and a big forest here, the Wiener Wald.

Vienna was named world's most liveable city in 2022 1 and I love living here. On the other hand Vienna was also ranked the world's unfriendliest city 2.

Do you want to know more about Vienna? Have a look at the Wikipedia article 3.
